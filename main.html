Merida, the fearless princess of the Scottish Highlands, refuses to follow the traditions set for her. Skilled with a bow and driven by a desire to control her own destiny, she challenges an ancient custom that brings chaos to her kingdom. After a magical spell goes terribly wrong, Merida must rely on her courage, bravery, and determination to break the curse and mend the bond with her mother before it’s too late. Brave is a story of adventure, family, and discovering what it truly means to be courageous.
